How to Play Movie Alchemy 🎬

  • 🧩 Drag two items into the workspace to try combining them.
  • 🔁 Only two elements can be combined at a time.
  • 💾 Use the buttons on the left:
    • Clear – clears the canvas.
    • Save – saves your progress to this device.
    • Load – restores your last save.
    • Trash – drag items here to remove them.
  • 🚫 Elements in italic purple are terminal — they can't be combined any further.
