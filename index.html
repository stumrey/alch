How to Play 🎬

  • Combine 2 elements in the workspace to create new ones.
  • Drag from inventory into the workspace to test combos.
  • Only two elements can be combined at once.
  • 🧬 Terminal elements (purple italics) can't combine with anything else.
  • 🔤 Use A–Z button to sort inventory. Tap again to reverse.
  • 🧬 Use terminal toggle to hide/show terminal elements.
  • 🗑️ Drag items into the bin to remove from workspace.
